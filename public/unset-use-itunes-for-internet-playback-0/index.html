Unset Use iTunes for Internet Playback

Jun 28th, 2010

You know when you clicked on the option to use iTunes as the default player for Internet media and immediately regretted it well I’ve found the solution.

It seems the preferences pane option is not a toggle so you’ll need to go in an edit the file associates yourself.

Rubicode - RCDefaultApp is the answer to your prayers

http://www.rubicode.com/Software/RCDefaultApp/

it adds a preference pane into Systems Preferences to give you full control and restore order.

or… as @danlane pointed out to me after posting this originally
you could right click on a file, choose “Get Info” then set “Open With” remembering to hit the “Change All…”Â 
But I think my way preserves the feeling of magic I like from an OS.
